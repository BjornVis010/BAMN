Welcome to our official website!

What do we do?

We can't say everything, but we don't only do music.
We also sell merchandise, connect to the kids and eldery and we make a party out of our lives.
Some of us also have regular jobs, so some of us really help society!

Who are we?

We are BAMN, what stands for Boys Are Men Now.
We got 4 men in our group, those are:
- Fishie
- NIES
- Mitchell van Hengel
- Nodegro

Fishie:

These guys really changed my life. I never had good friends, or I got betrayed by them.
Then these guys came into my life and we knew we had to do something with eatchother.
Mitch released his first single, and I was working on one. I just heared Nodegro wanted to follow his music-heart,
And NIES and I were already thinking about making music together. that's when I realised:
We had to form a group. A label. the possibility to make your own music, but also to make music with eatchother.
now, we all have something on the table and we are doing shit next to music!
It's crazy how it turned out, I knew we had a change, but I didn't know we could grow this fast. Thank YOU!
